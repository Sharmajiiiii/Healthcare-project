Coronary Artery Disease (CAD)

CAD is the most common heart problem. With CAD you may get blockages in your coronary arteries(the vessels that supply blood to your heart). Coronary heart disease can give you pain in your chest, called angina, or lead to a heart attack.

Symptoms of Diseases.

  • Chest pain
  • Burning
  • Shortness of breath
  • Abnormal heart rhythm
  • Heaviness

Precautions of diseases

  • Limiting alcohol intake
  • Maintaining a healthy body weight
  • Maintaining healthy cholesterol levels
  • Undertaking regular physical exercise
  • Eating a balanced diet
