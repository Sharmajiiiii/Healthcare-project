Acute Cerebellar Ataxia(ACA

ACA is a disorder that occurs when the cerebellum becomes inflamed or damaged. The cerebellar is the area of the brain responsible for controlling gait and muscle coordination.

Symptoms of Diseases.

  • Impaired coordination in legs and arms
  • Headaches and vocal change
  • Slurred speech
  • Uncontrolled or repetitive eye movement
  • Dizziness and frequent stumbling

Precautions of diseases
