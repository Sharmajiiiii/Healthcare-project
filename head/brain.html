Brain Cancer

Brain cancer is an overgrowth of cells in your brain that forms masses called tumors. Brain cancer can grow very quickly, depending on the type of tumor.

Symptoms of Diseases.

  • Headaches usually worse in morning
  • A lack of balance and coordination
  • Vomiting
  • Difficulty in walking, talking and thinking
  • Vision and Speech problem
  • Memory lapses and personality changes

Precautions of diseases

  • Avoiding Smoking and alcohol
  • Avoid exposure to pesticides, insecticides
  • Avoid unnecessary exposure to radiation
  • Avoid exposure to carcinogenic chemicals
